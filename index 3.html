About Us

Welcome to Pathway Luxury Suites

Our short term and long term furnished rental condos are located in the heart of downtown Mississauga. From the corporate traveler, to the vacationing family to the individual looking to relocate to the city, the individualized style of the company is ideally suited for those looking for that personal touch.

Each property is fully equipped to meet all our clients' needs. We offer a unique solution for those looking for an alternative to costly hotel stays and provide superb accommodations at a reasonable price.

With an offering of luxurious, high quality furnished suites supported by great customer service, Pathway Suites places client satisfaction above all else. Our exciting portfolio has been designed with quality, comfort, convenience, privacy and security in mind, each unit selectively handpicked based on location and individually designed with particular attention to detail.
